Game Instructions: 
Player    (Yellow)    Press "spacebar" to possess Enemies that are in your line of sight.    
Enemies   (Red)       Press "r" to restart level.                                               
Exit      (Green)     Press "esc" to exit to main menu.                                        
Obstacles (Blue)      Note: Player takes on Enemy initial during possession  
                   
Player lose ability to possess when they are already possessing enemies.                         #
Player can only possess new enemy by changing back to "M"                                        #
To turn back to original form, press "c".


 Obstacles (Blue)   'd' is a destroyable obstacle that can only be destroyed by (R)hino.         #
 To destroy obstacle 'd', press "Spacebar" while facing obstacle 'd'.                            #
 (R)hino can only destroy up to 2 'd' before automatically turning back to (M)onkey.
